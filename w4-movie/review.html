The Master Of Disguise

1000 Faces...And Not A Single Clue

ABOUT CAST REVIEW

Review

Tony Rodriguez

Febuary 5,2023

Dana Carvey widely known for his appearances on Saturday Night Live. Does a wonderful job of capturing the highlights of each charcater played in this film. From impressions of Al Pacino's Tony Montana in Scarface to Robert Shaw's Quint from Jaws. With a character for everyone there is no doubt you will love The Master Of Disguise. If you are into adventure comedies with unexpected fart jokes and more impressions then a nutty proffesor film then your going to love this film.

Trivia

  • Dana Carvey played the drums himself for Pistachio's turtle character during the credits.
  • The song that Pistachio plays on the recorder while disguised as the Indian prince is Songbird by Kenny G.
  • The reason why so many people get slapped in the face in this film is because PG rated movies can't have too much face punching. If people get punched, their faces must be covered which is why Pistachio inexplicably fights ninjas as the end of the film.
