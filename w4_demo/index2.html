Movie Description

Pistachio Disgisey (Dana Carvey) a sweet-mild mannered waiter, working at Grandfather Disgisey (Harold Gould) Fabrizio's italian Restaurant.

In a world of magic and extrodinary power Pistachio must become something more. With his newly tapped skill he must solve the mystery of his parents dissapearence .

Best Known for his Skits on Saturday Night Live quick Impressions.He is Wonderful at impersonating well known stars like Jimmy Stewart, George Bush, and Johnny Carson.

His long list of immaculate impressions, makes it an absolute joy of a movie to watch with something for everyone.

With a movie aimed at young and mature crowed. Silly puns and unexpected fart jokes no doubt you will absolutely like this movie .
