Homer Simpson

Role: Nuclear Safety Inspector

Bio: Homer is the lovable but often clueless safety inspector at the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant.

Marge Simpson

Role: Homemaker

Bio: Marge is the loving and patient wife of Homer Simpson, dedicated to her family.

Barney Gumble

Role: Duff Brewery Worker

Bio: Barney is a frequent patron of Moe's Tavern and occasionally works at the Duff Brewery.

Chief Wiggum

Role: Police Chief

Bio: Chief Wiggum is the often incompetent but well-meaning police chief of Springfield.

Apu Nahasapeemapetilon

Role: Kwik-E-Mart Owner

Bio: Apu is the friendly owner of the Kwik-E-Mart convenience store in Springfield.

Sideshow Bob

Role: Former Krusty the Clown Sidekick

Bio: Sideshow Bob, formerly Krusty the Clown's sidekick, is known for his intelligence and vendetta against Bart Simpson.

Ned Flanders

Role: Owner of The Leftorium

Bio: Ned Flanders is the friendly neighbor and devout Christian known for his catchphrase "Okily Dokily."

Moe Szyslak

Role: Bar Owner (Moe's Tavern)

Bio: Moe Szyslak is the gruff yet lovable owner and bartender of Moe's Tavern.

Edna Krabappel

Role: Fourth Grade Teacher

Bio: Edna Krabappel is the sarcastic and cynical fourth-grade teacher at Springfield Elementary School.

Dr. Hibbert

Role: Springfield's Respected Doctor

Bio: Dr. Julius Hibbert is Springfield's friendly and highly competent doctor, known for his distinctive laugh.

Fat Tony

Role: Mob Boss

Bio: Fat Tony is the mob boss of the Springfield Mafia, known for his calm demeanor and criminal enterprises.

Principal Seymour Skinner

Role: Principal of Springfield Elementary School

Bio: Principal Skinner is the strict and by-the-book principal of Springfield Elementary School. Despite his stern exterior, he cares about the well-being of the students and often finds himself entangled in the chaos caused by Bart Simpson.

Groundskeeper Willie

Role: Groundskeeper at Springfield Elementary School

Bio: Groundskeeper Willie is the Scottish caretaker at Springfield Elementary School. He takes care of the school grounds and is known for his gruff personality and love for his tractor.

Reverend Lovejoy

Role: Minister at the First Church of Springfield

Bio: Reverend Lovejoy is the minister at the First Church of Springfield. He is often seen as indifferent and weary due to the antics of his parishioners, including the Simpson family.

Selma Bouvier

Role: DMV Employee

Bio: Selma Bouvier is one of Marge Simpson's older twin sisters. She works at the Springfield Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV) and is known for her chain smoking and unsuccessful relationships.

Patty Bouvier

Role: DMV Employee

Bio: Patty Bouvier is Marge Simpson's other older twin sister. Like Selma, she also works at the Springfield Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV) and shares similar traits, including a cynical outlook on life.

Mr. Burns

Role: Owner of the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant

Bio: Mr. Burns is the wealthy and elderly owner of the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant. He is known for his extreme wealth, callous demeanor, and his loyal assistant, Waylon Smithers.

Waylon Smithers

Role: Executive Assistant to Mr. Burns

Bio: Waylon Smithers is the devoted executive assistant to Mr. Burns. He is often seen trying to fulfill Mr. Burns' every need and is known for his unwavering loyalty, despite Mr. Burns' often indifferent attitude.
